My Travel App

The process of creating my travel app

In this project I created a childrens travel app to inform them of the different types of animals in the rainforest.

Always start on Paper

I firstly drew a mindmap to come up with ideas for my travel app. I came to the conclusion that I wanted to make a travel app based on animals in the rainforest. I then decided to target it at children to provide them with facts about the different types of animals there are.

Digitalising

I then digitalised my designs. I created wireframes so I had an idea of what I wanted my app to look like. I then used sketch to finalise the design of my app.
